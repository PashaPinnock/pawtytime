About Us

Pet Party Planners was founded by a group of passionate animal lovers with one goal: to bring joy to pets and their owners through unforgettable celebrations. We understand how special your pet is to you, and we strive to make every event as memorable as possible.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide pets with the best parties ever! We aim to create an enjoyable experience for both pets and their owners by offering unique party themes, catering, entertainment, and decorations.

Meet the Team

Our team is made up of pet lovers, event planners, and creative individuals who work hard to ensure your pet’s event is perfect.

Founder: Emma Johnson

Emma is the visionary behind Pet Party Planners. As a lifelong pet lover and professional event planner, she wanted to combine her passions into something special for pets and their families.

Co-Founder: Olivia Carter

Olivia co-founded Pet Party Planners with Emma, bringing her expertise in customer relations and creative design. She believes that every pet deserves a party that celebrates their unique personality.

Our Values

  • Care: We treat every pet like our own.
  • Creativity: We offer unique and personalized party themes.
  • Fun: Our parties are all about joy, laughter, and creating lasting memories.
  • Safety: We ensure that every pet’s safety is our top priority during their event.
